 OG (Orthologous Groups)

OG (Orthologous Groups) were defined based on the combined information between eggNOG annotations and NCBI KOG database.

NCBI provides a structured information of COGs and Eukaryotic clusters (KOGs) in Conserved Domain Database (CDD).

 Sequence alignment

Protein sequence alignment is constructed from the protein sequences of 75 representative fungal species. Sequences are visualized with MSAViewer plugin, using Clustal2 color format.

Note that the sequences are not complete, since gap-rich columns with 0.5 threshold from the alignment were hidden before visualization.
